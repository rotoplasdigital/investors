Carlos Rojas Mota Velasco

Excecutive President | Chairman of the Board

Carlos Rojas Mota Velasco is the co-founder, Executive President, and Chairman of the Board of Directors of Grupo Rotoplas, the leading provider of individual and integrated water solutions in the Americas.

Carlos has long been recognized for his entrepreneurial spirit, having participated in a wide range of industries both as investor and advisor.

He is an active philanthropist and serves as Executive President and Chairman of the Board of The Mexican Foundation for Education, Technology and Science (FUNED, AC), a non-profit organization that offers Mexican students financial support to pursue graduate studies overseas.

He also serves on the Boards of Directors of Scribe, Nasoft, Universidad Centro and Grupo Lar.

Carlos holds a degree in Business Administration from Universidad Anáhuac, CDMX, MX.

Carlos Rojas Aboumrad

CEO | Director

Carlos Rojas joined Grupo Rotoplas in 2004 through the Company’s Executive Development Program while still enrolled at university. He was appointed as Grupo Rotoplas’ Chief New Business Officer and joined the Rotoplas Board of Directors in 2014. He is the Group’s CEO since May 2019.

Since joining the Company, he has held various operational positions within the organization; he actively contributed in the Agua para Todos program in Brazil, boosted the Innovation and Development area growth, led the United States expansion, and executed the water fountain program in Mexico.

Carlos has identified valuable opportunities for the company, including strategies focused on the sustainable development of the organization.

Carlos holds a degree in Industrial Engineering from Instituto Tecnológico de Estudios Superiores de Monterrey, Santa Fe campus, CDMX, MX, and a Master's in Business Administration from Babson College, MA, USA.

Mario Romero

CFO | Director

Mario Romero joined Grupo Rotoplas in 1995 through the Company’s Executive Development Program while still enrolled at university. He was appointed as Grupo Rotoplas’ Chief Financial Officer in 2004 and joined the Rotoplas Board of Directors in 2003.

Over the last 10 years, Mario has held various operational positions with a particular focus on finance and strategy. During this time, he also opened Rotoplas’ operations in Peru and Brazil, launched the Company’s filters and pipes businesses in Mexico and established its resin compounding facility.

His day-to-day work involves making decisions that have a financial impact on the Company, efficient resource management, and approval of the annual financial and sustainability report, among others.

Mario holds a Bachelor’s degree in Economics from the Instituto Tecnológico Autónomo de Mexico (ITAM), CDMX, MX, and a Graduate Certificate in Business Administration from Harvard University, MA, EUA.

Gonzalo Uribe

COO | Director

Gonzalo Uribe has been COO since 2011. He joined Grupo Rotoplas in 2001 and joined the Rotoplas Board of Directors in 2013.

Gonzalo has held various operational positions since joining the Company, and has been involved in the implementation of the Supply Chain and the pipe business growth, positioning it as the market leader. His leadership has been fundamental for the international consolidation and growth of the business in recent years.

He is in charge of improving the Supply Chain processes, optimizing resources, and implementing communication strategies that raise client awareness about responsible and sustainable water use, among others.

Before joining Grupo Rotoplas he was Chief Operation Officer at Comexma SA de CV, a textile business.

He holds a degree in Industrial Engineering form the Universidad Anáhuac, CDMX, MX and a Master's in Business Administration from the Instituto Panamericano de Alta Dirección de Empresas (IPADE), CDMX, MX.

Pablo Iturbe

Director

Xavier García de Quevedo

Consejero Independiente

Es Ingeniero Químico de la UNAM, con estudios en Administración y Finanzas por el ITESM. Actualmente es Vicepresidente del Consejo y Vicepresidente Ejecutivo de Grupo México que tiene tres sectores de negocios, Minería, Transporte Ferroviario/ Intermodal e Infraestructura. Xavier inició su carrera profesional en Bufete Industrial en 1969 y unos meses después inicia su carrera con Grupo México como Ingeniero de Proyectos y de Operaciones.

En Grupo México ha ocupado diversos puestos: Director General de Desarrollo del Grupo, Presidente y fundador de Grupo Ferroviario Mexicano (Ferromex), Presidente de Asarco Inc en los Estados Unidos, Presidente de Minera México, Presidente de Americas Mining (Holding de la División Minera), Presidente y Fundador de la División Infraestructura y Director de Administración y Finanzas de Grupo México.

Colaboró en Grupo Condumex de 1986 a 1994, siendo responsable de la creación de la División Intermodal, de la Constructora de Telecomunicaciones “Procisa” y posteriormente ocupo los cargos de Director Comercial y Director de las Divisiones de Telecomunicaciones y cable automotriz.

Es miembro de los Consejos de Administración de Grupo México, Americas Mining Corporation, Southern Copper Corporation, Grupo Mexico Transportes y Ferromex. Xavier fue Presidente de la Cámara Minera de México de Noviembre de 2006 a Agosto de 2009.
